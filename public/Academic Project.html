Academic Project

This showcase 3 of the selected academic projects during my university, which believe best representing my technical skillsets.

  • Infrastructural Ecotone Next Generation Airport

    RMIT major project 2016

    This is an experimental project that explores what happens to the city and border condition when you inject large infrastructural processor into dense urban environment. In responding to the above questions, I am proposing an architecture that operates at both urban and building scale, has this blurred spatial relationship, concurrent access from all different sides instead of the traditional rigidly bifurcated sequential experience into the building; multiple trajectory throughout the building; complex event flow in terms of how people move through for different type of events; control of access visual or physical permeable; circulation space becomes sport events accommodated

  • Layering Mentone Suburban Multi-Use Residential

    RMIT design studio project 2016

    As an alternative to the traditional top down planning, the project adopts the strategy of superposition and layering of uses, proposes the modified suburb house as a source of regeneration that provides new housing stock whilst absorbing activities within its new type and urban fabric.

    Shopping centres are a hybrid civic space where more than shopping can happen; they are the core of a suburban village. This hybrid is achieved by the containment of retail space, engaging socially with community life and forcing a more dispersed flow of interaction.

  • Pheromone Contouring Robotics Research

    RMIT design elective project 2015

    The project explores autonomous systems for real time robotic fabrication, which seeks to imbue physical material with a new digital agency using real time robotic control and computer vision systems. There are 3 main branches of development that have came from our body of work: stigmergy as environmental feedback, the slow robotics paradigm and expanded vision systems.
